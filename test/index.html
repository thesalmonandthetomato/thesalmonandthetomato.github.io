The Salmon and the Tomato

Tracing the impacts of everyday food

The Salmon and The Tomato is a multimedia project that explores the hidden connections between global food systems, from salmon farms in Northern Europe to greenhouse agriculture in southern Spain.

Through investigative journalism, visual storytelling, research, and creative communication, the project examines the ecological, social, and economic impacts of intensive food production.

We combine data-driven studies, migrant narratives, photoessays, short films, and educational materials to reveal the consequences of industrialised supply chains while inviting reflection on sustainable alternatives.

This is a project where evidence meets artistry, uncovering the systems behind what we eat and the stories often left untold.
